Something About

The blog site was made with Hugo theme hermit one of the most popular open-source static site generators.

The blog comment system was made with Valine a fast, simple & powerful comment system.

Valine is used in Gravatar as commentator avatar.

  • Login Gravatar to upload a picture as you avatar.
  • When you leave comment just key your Gravatar register email into email box.
  • Valine 支持 qq 头像, 在留言板邮箱栏输入 qq邮箱 [例如12345678@qq.com] .
  • Then your avatar will auto display with your comments.
